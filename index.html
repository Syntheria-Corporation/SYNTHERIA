Our Strategic Horizon

This dynamic roadmap outlines our methodical approach to building SYNTHERIA into a highly capitalized private corporation. Each phase is a distinct step, systematically progressing from foundational setup to significant asset growth and potential future strategic evolutions. Scroll through to observe our journey.

Phase 1

Short-Term (0-18 Mo)

  • Secure CAD $100,000 seed funding to establish our core ETH reserve.
  • Acquire foundational ETH and immediately begin staking to initiate revenue generation.
  • Establish and audit a secure, robust validator infrastructure for our private holdings.
  • Ensure full regulatory compliance as a private entity.
  • Systematically reinvest early returns to accelerate ETH reserve growth.
Phase 2

Medium-Term (3-5 Yr)

  • Significantly grow the corporate ETH reserve through aggressive reinvestment.
  • Optimize validator operations and infrastructure to maximize yield and efficiency.
  • Potentially explore offering network-securing services to select external clients.
  • Solidify the stability and independence of our sales-free revenue model.
Phase 3

Long-Term (5+ Yr)

  • Become a highly capitalized private corporation, a recognized blockchain contributor.
  • Evaluate strategic benefits of becoming a publicly traded company.
  • If public, implement a formal dividend distribution policy.
  • Explore diversification into staking other prominent Proof-of-Stake assets.
  • Contribute to the broader blockchain ecosystem through research and innovation.

Fortifying the Future: Risk & Mitigation

Every venture involves inherent risks, and a robust strategy acknowledges and addresses them proactively. This section outlines the key challenges we've identified and our comprehensive mitigation plans. Click on each card to reveal our approach to safeguarding SYNTHERIA's future.

Market Risk

Volatility in ETH price and staking yields.

Click to see mitigation

Mitigation Plan

  • Focus on accumulating more ETH, as revenue is ETH-denominated.
  • Communicate long-term value over short-term price swings.
  • Future dividend policy to account for yield fluctuations.
▨

Operational Risk

Security breaches, slashing penalties, and downtime.

Click to see mitigation

Mitigation Plan

  • Multi-layer security: cold storage, HSMs.
  • Regular third-party security audits.
  • Redundant infrastructure to prevent downtime and slashing.
  • Slashing insurance/reserve fund.
